The Best Movies According to Cassie

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

Everything, Everywhere, All At Once

This film makes me laugh and cry within the same scenes. I relate to Evelyn's collection of failed hobbies, embrace it's philosophy of cheerful nihilism, and I aspire to fight with kindness like Waymond. While I connect to it deeply on a personal level, it's an excellently casted and crafted film with just my style of artsy silliness.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

I don't think this counts as a favorite "movie," but I can't pick just one. I believe this is the greatest series of films ever made. It's also personally special to myself and my husband.

Amelie

It's basically a perfect film. An artistically shot film about shyness, kindness, and two weirdos finding each other in a whimsical and idealized Paris.

Honorable Mentions

Labyrinth

While there are other films of higher quality, this is a special childhood favorite that forever influenced my tastes. I would give it my personal lifetime achievement award.

Underrated Favorites

October Sky, Pleasantville, Gattaca. I never hear people talk about these but they are excellent.

My "Morning Movies"

"Morning Movies" is my term for calm, comforting films perfect for slow mornings and stressful times. While there are several, I've easily watched these films the most: Jane Eyre (2011), Pride and Prejudice (2005), Little Women (2019), Ever After, and Practical Magic.
